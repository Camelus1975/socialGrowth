# App Founder Growth Suite - User Manual

Welcome to the **App Founder Growth Suite**! This platform is your all-in-one "mission control" for marketing, managing, and scaling your software application or startup. You don't need to be a technical expert to use this tool. This manual will guide you through the features and how to use them effectively.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

When you open the app, you will see a navigation menu on the left side of your screen (the sidebar). From here, you can switch between different "Views" or modules. Each module is designed to handle a specific part of your app's growth strategy.

At the very top left, you can switch between the different apps you are managing using the dropdown menu.

---

## 🗺️ Core Modules & Features

### 1. Launch Command Center
**What it is:** The place to plan your app launches or new feature releases.
**How to use it:** 
- Type what you want to launch (e.g., "Launch my new habit tracking app") and click **Generate Launch Assets**.
- The AI will instantly create a "Strategic Blueprint", a "Timeline", and a "Content Calendar" with recommended social media posts to build hype leading up to launch day.

### 2. Marketing Engine
**What it is:** The central hub for creating, scheduling, and managing social media posts across all platforms (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, etc.).
**How to use it:**
- Type a prompt or idea into the "AI Post Copilot" and click **Generate Draft**. The AI will write a ready-to-publish social media post.
- You can preview how the post will look on different platforms (like X or LinkedIn) using the tabs.
- Set a date and time, and click **Schedule & Add to Queue** to automatically post it later.

### 3. Unified Inbox
**What it is:** A single place to read and reply to all comments, direct messages, and brand mentions from Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
**How to use it:**
- Click on any message in the list to view the conversation.
- Type your reply at the bottom and hit **Send Reply**. 
- Click the **AI Suggest** button if you want the system to generate a polite, professional reply for you automatically!

### 4. Assets & Media Hub
**What it is:** Your secure cloud storage for app screenshots, logos, videos, and marketing graphics.
**How to use it:**
- Click **Upload Media File** to select an image or video from your computer.
- Once uploaded, the file will appear in your gallery.
- You can copy the link to any image to use it in your social media posts or campaigns.

### 5. Content Intelligence
**What it is:** The analytics dashboard. It tells you what is working and what isn't.
**How to use it:**
- Check your **Total Reach**, **Conversions**, and **Growth** numbers at the top.
- Look at the **Best Times to Post** and **Top Hashtags** to understand when and how to post for maximum engagement based on your historical data.

### 6. Advertising Network
**What it is:** A place to manage your paid ad campaigns (like Meta/Facebook Ads or Google Ads).
**How to use it:**
- Enter a daily budget and target audience, then click **Generate AI Ad Creative**.
- The system will create an image and ad copy for you.
- Once you are happy with the ad, click **Approve & Launch Campaign** to send it live.

### 7. ASO & Product Strategy
**What it is:** App Store Optimization (ASO). This helps your app rank higher in the Apple App Store and Google Play Store when people search for keywords.
**How to use it:**
- Review the AI-suggested keywords, search volumes, and competition levels.
- Use these keywords in your app's title and description in the App Store to get more organic (free) downloads.

### 8. Roadmap & Release
**What it is:** A public-facing roadmap where you can show your users what features you are building next.
**How to use it:**
- Add new features to the "In Progress" or "Planned" columns.
- Click **Toggle Public Roadmap** to make it visible to your users.
- When you finish a feature, the AI can automatically write a "Release Notes" post for your social media.

### 9. Content Recycler & Copilot
**What it is:** A tool to turn one piece of content (like a blog post or a long video) into dozens of smaller social media posts.
**How to use it:**
- Paste a link to a blog post or YouTube video and click **Recycle Content**.
- The AI will summarize the link and create Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, and short quotes from it.

### 10. Auth & Team Settings
**What it is:** The administrative dashboard where you manage who has access to your Growth Suite.
**How to use it:**
- See a list of everyone in your organization (Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer).
- Click **Invite Member** to send an email invitation to a new team member.
- Click **Remove** to revoke access for someone who no longer works with you.

---

## ⚙️ How to Connect Your Accounts

To get the most out of the app, you need to connect your real social media accounts (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram) and your App Store developer accounts.

1. Go to the **Auth & Team Settings** page.
2. In the Integrations section, look for the social media platforms.
3. Click **Connect** next to Twitter, LinkedIn, etc., and sign in to grant the app permission to post on your behalf.

---

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

- **"AI is not generating content":** Ensure your OpenAI API key is properly configured in the Railway hosting dashboard.
- **"Mock data is showing":** If you are a brand new user, some pages (like Content Intelligence) will show empty/zero states until you actually generate and publish posts.
- **Need help?** Reach out to your technical administrator or system owner for access issues.
